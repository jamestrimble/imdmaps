Indices of Deprivation for UK Countries

This site contains interactive maps of deprivation in England, Scotland and Wales, using officially-published data.

Interactive Maps

  • English Indices of Deprivation 2015
  • Scottish Index of Multiple Deprivation (SIMD) 2016 (Previous release: SIMD 2012)
  • Welsh Index of Multiple Deprivation (WIMD) 2014
  • Note: maps of the Northern Ireland Multiple Deprivation Measure 2010 are available from NISRA

About this site

This site is a personal project by James Trimble. Thanks to Alasdair Rae (University of Sheffield) and Maike Waldmann (Scottish Government) for helpful comments on drafts of the maps.

I would love to receive feedback on how you use these maps or how they could be improved: contact james dot trimble at yahoo dot co dot uk .
